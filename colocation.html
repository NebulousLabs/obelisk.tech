COLOCATION (COMING SOON)

Obelisk represents a unique opportunity for those interested in the benefits that come from securing the Sia network through the power of ASICs. We realize though, that not everyone has a suitable space that can handle the electrical and cooling requirements of mining hardware, especially when operating more than one or two units.

DATA CENTERS / COLOCATION

The main alternative to home operation is to find a data center (also sometimes called a colocation facility or "colo") where your Obelisks can operate. It can be quite time consuming to find a good facility that understands the needs of miners, provides "remote hands" services if your Obelisks need attention, and offers the service at a good price.

OBELISK RECOMMENDED COLOCATION

We want owning and operating your Obelisks to be an easy, fun and rewarding experience, so we've done the work for you! Below you will find a list of recommended colocation packages at companies with facilities across North America and Europe.

One major benefit of selecting a recommended colocation package is that your Obelisks will be shipped directly to your chosen provider. They will receive your Obelisks, unpack them, connect them to power & internet, then send you an email with information on how to login remotely so you can configure your mining pool and other settings and start mining.
